About

What Was
The Boweress?

The Boweress was a Sydney-based digital platform and content studio, founded and directed by Lucy Pero. Operating from 2016 to 2019, it celebrated gender parity, challenged stereotypes, and forged positive visibility for women — highlighting their achievements across creative, commercial, and activist spheres.

The name comes from Medieval England. The Bower was a private room within a castle — an attractive withdrawing space intended for the lady of the household. The Boweress reimagined that idea for the contemporary moment: the personal havens women create for themselves, for reflection as much as creation, for collaboration as much as solitude.

“We believe there is a symbiotic relationship between a connection to habitat and a sense of collaboration.”

Beyond its editorial and community work, the studio produced content and events for select clients — from global automotive brands to local food heroes. This site is a faithful archive of everything published before the platform went quiet.

What The Boweress Did

From strategy to shoot, the studio delivered across the full content spectrum — for brands that wanted creative work with cultural intelligence built in.

Video Production

End-to-end video production including shoot coordination, direction, and post-production. Films premiered at experiential events and distributed across digital channels.

Long-form features, campaign copy, EDM content, and brand narratives. A strong editorial sensibility ran through every piece of writing produced.

Experiential & Events

Content coverage and creative production for live events, festival activations, and brand launches — including MINI at Dark Mofo and Hendricks Gin in Sydney.

Web Design & Development

End-to-end digital presence — from concept and design through to build and launch. The Boweress platform itself is a testament to the studio’s full-stack creative capability.

Art & Creative Direction

Brand and creative consultation, visual identity, and art direction across shoots, campaigns, and brand partnerships. Where the idea becomes the visual.

The Founder

Lucy Pero

Visual Arts educator, Pilates instructor, and the creative director who built The Boweress.

Lucy holds a Masters of Teaching in Visual Arts and a Bachelor of Arts in Art History and Theory, both from the University of Sydney — the academic foundation that ran through every interview, feature, and creative brief The Boweress ever produced.

She has spent the past decade teaching across Sydney’s independent school sector. Since 2020 she has been at MLC School Sydney, first in the junior school and from 2023 as a full-time Visual Arts Teacher, drawing on her deep background in Art History and Theory to support and challenge students across K–12. In 2025 she stepped into the role of Acting Head of Department.

Beyond the classroom, Lucy is a certified Pilates instructor (Elixir School, Reformer and Mat) and the founder of P1 Pilates — an online platform offering mat classes for all levels, with a longer ambition of building tailored wellness programs for educators and students.

The thread connecting The Boweress to what came after it is easy to find: a commitment to amplifying others, building communities, and making things that last.
